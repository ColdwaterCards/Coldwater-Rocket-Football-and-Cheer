About Us

Welcome to Coldwater Rocket Football and Cheerleading! We are dedicated to providing a safe and fun environment for youth athletes to learn, grow, and compete in football and cheerleading. Our coaches are passionate about teaching skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Our Programs

Football

We offer competitive football teams for students in grades 3 through 6. Practices are held during the week with games on Saturdays.

Cheerleading

Our cheerleading program supports our football teams and builds confidence, coordination, and spirit in all our athletes.
